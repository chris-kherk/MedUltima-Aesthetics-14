What is Emsculpt Neo Treatment?

Emsculpt Neo is an innovative body sculpting treatment combining radiofrequency (RF) heating with high-intensity focused electromagnetic (HIFEM) technology. How Does Emsculpt Neo Work?

The treatment uses two advanced technologies: RF (Radio Frequency) energy and HIFEM (High-Intensity Focused Electromagnetic) pulses. First, the RF energy gently warms up your tissues to prepare them for the intense muscle contractions caused by the HIFEM technology. These powerful contractions are much stronger than you can achieve through regular exercise, helping your muscles grow and reducing fat simultaneously.

This combination of RF energy and HIFEM pulses improves muscle tone and decreases fat in specific body areas. It's a noninvasive way to enhance your body shape and achieve results that are hard to achieve with exercise alone.
